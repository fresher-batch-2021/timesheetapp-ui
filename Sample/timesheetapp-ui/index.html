Barcode scanner

Instructions:

  • Click to view any barcode and point your screen towards the camera. You should hear a beep!
  • After that, Once the border on the right is red, the scanner is ready for another barcode.
  • If you're trying to scan a barcode and the border on the right is still green, move the barcode out of the camera view and back again.

Important notes:

  • Make sure to allow the browser to access your camera.
